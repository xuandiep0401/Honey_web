Our Story

Honey Haven began in 2010 when our family started caring for wild bee colonies deep in the Blue Ridge Mountains. We believe in sustainable beekeeping, letting each hive thrive naturally. From flower to jar, our honey is raw, unfiltered, and bursting with flavor.
